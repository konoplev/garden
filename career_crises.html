Career crises

Oct 9, 2020 #

What is crises

Understanding that you don’t want to live in the same way as you lived before.

2 reasons

There are 3 reasons of having a career crises:

  1. You understand that you have a great potential and it’s not applied.

  2. You understand that your expectations and your live are very different.

    • You found someone who is a role model for you

    • You are in a situation that changes your life. You start thinking what you achieved and you don’t have something.

Survivorship bias

When you hear about ideal careers you can start thinking that your career should be changed. But this is how to want to become a person from advertisment.

Nothing is ideal. We just don’t hear about mistakes and problems.

Everyone has mistakes and everyone has problems. You can’t be happy 100% of time. So it’s ok to be not perfect.
